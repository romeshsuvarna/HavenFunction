About

Haven Function Center & Restaurant was created for all kinds of events such as birthdays, graduations, baptisms, first communions, quinceaneras and weddings. Our mission statement is to provide all customers and customers' guests by offering exclusive ameneties in the safest and most enjoyable and professional manner possible.

We want your events to be a success! Our team will provide you and your guests with any assistance you may need and provide any further assistance to ensure your event is a total success from beginning to end. Haven is the best hall to host your next event, and is a fun place for kids, all family and adults. We have the perfect party package for any event at an affordable cost! We have a host of fun services and amenities such as unlimited air hockey, karaoke, jumping balloons and a lot more! Our competitive rates will allow you and your loved ones to fully benefit from all our services and activities.
